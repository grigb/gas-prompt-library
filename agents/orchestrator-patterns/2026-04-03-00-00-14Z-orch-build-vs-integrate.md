# P-014: Build vs. Integrate Decision

**Action:** Research whether to build from scratch or integrate/fork existing implementations. Document tradeoffs in the proposal.
**Why:** Building from scratch when a library exists wastes weeks. The AT Protocol PDS workstream saved 40-60 hours by choosing the hybrid approach.
**Examples:** New protocol integration -- research existing libraries, compare build-vs-integrate tradeoffs, recommend approach in proposal.
